Origin

This name originated during a borrel in a discussion between two German students, Sir Tim Weyhnes and Lady Pia Moznodowsky. The name was accepted without objections by the members of the whatsapp group formerly known as "Drinks". However, some sources suggest that the name's origin was a long time before that borrel and it was used by external parties to refer to the group.
